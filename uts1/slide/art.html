List of arts and culture in Singapadu

Here are some cultural heritages in Singapadu village that are still being preserved from generation to generation

The Peed Tradition in Singapadu

When you watch a mepeed in Singapadu which is held every six months, the people of Singapadu will wear clothes or payas agung combined with modern nuances with the local traditional village dress code.

Participants during the Mepeed tradition in Singapadu brought gebogan for PKK mothers and for young people, namely wearing great payas clothes combined with modern nuances.

Statue in Singapadu

Singapadu has a lot of sculptors, therefore the art of sculpting sculptures has become an economic source in Singapadu. If you pass around Jalan Raya Singapadu, you will see that on the right and left of the road, there will be lots of statues lined up.

Masks in Singapadu

As is the case with statues in Singapadu, there are also many mask-making artists, but masks in Singapadu have their own characteristics, namely having a sacred impression, so we, as young people in Singapadu, will always preserve the culture in our village.

Barong Dance Performance

Barong is something that is sacred to the people of Singapadu. In Singapadu, for the first time there was a Barong Kunti Sraya performance in Singapadu, Barong performances were also created which are now widely known in the world.
